SpaceX

SpaceX is a revolutionary aerospace company led by Elon Musk that aims to reduce space transportation costs and enable human colonization of Mars through innovations like reusable rockets.

Prologium

Prologium is an energy innovation company focused on lithium ceramic battery research, development, and manufacturing, that provides next-generation solid battery solutions for electric vehicles and non-EV in consumer markets and industrial applications.

China UnionPay

China UnionPay, one of the largest card payment organizations (debit and credit cards combined) in the world offering mobile and online payments based on total value of payment transactions. It is also the only interbank network in China linking all ATMs of all banks throughout the country, as well as the EFTPOS network.

SF Express

SF Express is a leading Chinese express delivery company renowned for its high-speed, reliable service, comprehensive logistics solutions, and advanced technology-enabled operations across domestic and international markets.
